College Principal

Dr. Amitabh Bachchan

Dr. Amitabh Bachchan is a visionary leader and the College Principal at Gurukul College of Defence and Civil Services. With a wealth of experience in both academia and the entertainment industry, Dr. Bachchan brings a unique perspective to the institution.

Under his leadership, the college has seen remarkable growth and innovation in its academic programs. Dr. Bachchan is known for his dedication to fostering a culture of excellence, creativity, and inclusivity among students and faculty.

His commitment to education and his charismatic leadership style have made him a beloved figure among the college community. Dr. Bachchan's vision for the college is to empower students to become not only academically proficient but also responsible and compassionate individuals ready to make a positive impact on society.
